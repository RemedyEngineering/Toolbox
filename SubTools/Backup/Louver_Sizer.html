Method

Sizing approach: 1. Free area required = Airflow (CFM) ÷ Max velocity (FPM) 2. Gross louver area = Free area ÷ free area ratio (from manufacturer) 3. Pick a trial louver (W × H), verify actual free-area velocity ≤ design limit. 4. ΔP estimate uses ΔP ≈ 0.30 × (V_free / 1000)² — planning value only. Always confirm against the selected manufacturer's published curve before issuing.
