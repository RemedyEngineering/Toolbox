Duct Sizing
Default drivers are Flow + Head Loss. The Duct Size row auto-fills at a 2:3 ratio (wider than high). Check the box beside Width or Height and edit the value to override that dimension — the other one auto-adjusts to keep the design intent. Check both to fully override; Head Loss then becomes a result.
