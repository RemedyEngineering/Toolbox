Method

Sizing approach: 1. Free area required = Airflow (CFM) ÷ design max velocity (FPM). 2. Gross louver area = Free area ÷ free-area ratio. For DE439/DE635 the ratio is interpolated from the published free-area table at the trial size; Custom lets you enter it. 3. Pick a trial louver (W × H). Actual free-area velocity = CFM ÷ trial free area. 4. ΔP — DE439/DE635 use the published air-performance coefficient: ΔP = K × (V_free / 4005)² (intake or exhaust K). Custom uses the planning estimate ΔP ≈ 0.30 × (V_free / 1000)². 5. Water penetration (intake) is a HARD STOP: the trial fails if free-area velocity reaches the model's beginning-of-water-penetration velocity (DE439 886 FPM, DE635 1,233 FPM), regardless of the other checks. Always confirm against the manufacturer's published curves before issuing.
